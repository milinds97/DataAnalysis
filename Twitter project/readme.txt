Wrangling & Analyzing WeRateDogs from Twitter

This folder contains the files for the project 4 on Wrangling & Analyzing a Twitter data of WeRateDogs.

Goal:
Gathering and combining multiple datasets of WeRateDogs from Twitter to create interesting and trustworthy analyses and visualizations. Although
maximum/all the datasets are already provided by the Udacity. Data needs to be programmatically assessing and cleaning for great analyses and visualizations.
