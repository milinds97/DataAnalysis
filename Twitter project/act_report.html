“WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog. It was started in 2015 by college student Matt Nelson and has received international media coverage both for its popularity and for the attention drawn to social media copyright law when it was suspended by Twitter.”, from Wikipedia. As of December 2017, the Twitter account has nearly 4.56 million followers.

The data, which was made available to me by Udacity as part of my Data Analyst Nanodegree program only included tweets up to August 1st, 2017 and were then filtered to remove retweets, tweets with dog breed missing and tweets where there was no image. The final usable dataset contained 1666 observations.
